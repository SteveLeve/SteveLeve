
# Professional Service Offering: Marketing Technology (MarTech) Consulting

## Service Description

This service is for businesses in the marketing and advertising technology space that need deep domain expertise to build, scale, or modernize their platforms. With 17 years of experience at ShareASale, a leading affiliate marketing network, we offer unparalleled insights into the MarTech landscape.

## Key Offerings

### 1. Affiliate & Partner Marketing Platform Development

*   **Situation:** You are building or scaling an affiliate marketing, partner, or influencer platform.
*   **Our Solution:** We provide architectural guidance and hands-on development support for building core MarTech features, including:
    *   **Tracking & Attribution:** Designing and implementing robust, scalable tracking systems for clicks, conversions, and commissions.
    *   **E-commerce Integration:** Building seamless integrations with e-commerce platforms like Shopify, Magento, and BigCommerce.
    *   **Communication & CRM:** Creating powerful communication tools, including newsletters, automated messaging, and CRM-like features for managing partner relationships.
    *   **Payments & Invoicing:** Developing secure and reliable systems for processing payments, managing invoices, and handling different fee structures.

### 2. AdTech & Campaign Management Systems

*   **Situation:** You need to build or improve your advertising technology, such as internal ad servers, campaign management tools, or video ad platforms.
*   **Our Solution:** We can help you design and build:
    *   **Ad Servers:** Creating systems for scheduling, targeting, and reporting on internal ad placements.
    *   **Campaign Management:** Developing tools for creating and managing marketing campaigns, including A/B testing, analytics, and optimization features.
    *   **Video Advertising:** Building platforms for hosting, serving, and creating interactive video ads.

### 3. MarTech Stack Audit & Modernization

*   **Situation:** Your existing MarTech platform is struggling to keep up with the demands of your business. It's slow, unreliable, or difficult to update.
*   **Our Solution:** We conduct a thorough audit of your MarTech stack and provide a roadmap for modernization. We can help you migrate to a more scalable and flexible architecture, improve performance, and reduce technical debt.
