
# Professional Service Offering: Enterprise Architecture (EA)

## Service Description

This service is for organizations that need to align their technology strategy with their business goals. We provide Enterprise Architecture (EA) expertise to help you create a clear vision for your technology landscape, manage your application portfolio, and govern your architecture to ensure that it supports your long-term objectives.

## Key Offerings

### 1. Technology Strategy & Roadmapping

*   **Situation:** You lack a clear technology strategy. Your teams are working in silos, and your technology investments are not aligned with your business priorities.
*   **Our Solution:** We work with your leadership team to develop a comprehensive technology strategy and roadmap. This includes defining your target architecture, identifying key initiatives, and creating a multi-year plan for achieving your goals.

### 2. Application Portfolio Management (APM)

*   **Situation:** Your application portfolio is a mess. You have redundant applications, outdated systems, and a lack of visibility into the total cost of ownership.
*   **Our Solution:** We help you rationalize your application portfolio. We conduct an inventory of your applications, assess their business value and technical health, and create a plan for retiring, retaining, rehosting, or refactoring each application.

### 3. Architecture Governance & Design Authority

*   **Situation:** You need to ensure that your architecture stays on track. You need a process for reviewing and approving architectural decisions to ensure that they are aligned with your strategy and standards.
*   **Our Solution:** We help you establish an architecture governance framework. This includes defining architectural principles and standards, creating an Architecture Review Board (ARB), and providing design authority for key projects.

### 4. M&A Technology Strategy & Integration

*   **Situation:** You are involved in a merger or acquisition, and you need to develop a strategy for integrating the technology landscapes of the two companies.
*   **Our Solution:** We provide EA expertise to support M&A activities. This includes conducting technical due diligence, developing a target architecture for the combined entity, and creating a plan for integrating applications, data, and infrastructure.
