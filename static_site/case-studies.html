Selected stories from 25+ years in technology: high-stakes reliability, pragmatic modernization, and measurable impact.

Data Center Exit under Austerity

Challenge: Looming data center closure, 20+ undocumented physical servers, high business risk.

Action: Reverse-engineered systems, scripted migrations, consolidated into VMware private cloud within 12 months.

Result: Eliminated existential risk, created foundation for AWS migration, no revenue loss.

AWS Migration & Observability

Challenge: Managed hosting bottleneck, performance and compliance pressures.

Action: Planned and executed a 6-month AWS migration with zero downtime; introduced Datadog/OpsGenie observability-first approach.

Result: Improved resilience, incident response time down 40%, featured in AWS case study.

Platform Consolidation

Challenge: Needed to merge ShareASale into parent platform, protect revenue streams during migration.

Action: Designed cross-system integrations (Cloudflare Workers + AWS Lambda), guided account/transaction migration.

Result: Protected 99.5% of revenue, migrated 250K publisher & 10K merchant accounts on schedule.
